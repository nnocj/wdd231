Overview

The name Dansoman Community Chamber of Commerce, represents the local community in which the chamber operates.

Purpose

The Dansoman Community Chamber of Commerce serves as catalyst for transformation of the area in to a strong business hub in the country.
We promote business and public policy that fosters economic prosperity within the area.

Frequently Asked Questions and Answers

Are there fees or dues?
Members pay 20 cedis a month to support the association in planing for meetings, and maintaining digital infrastructure.

What do I benefit from joining the chamber?
1. Network of Business owners and policy makers to reach out for support with your business 2. Share and receive new insights on latest developments and best practices 2. Contribute to positive community change in the areas 3. Receive alerts on business development news and meetings happening in the area
